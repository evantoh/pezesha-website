Our approach to meaningful financial inclusion is different

Hybrid Approach

We have agents all over who are embedded in our value chain to help us reach and understand our target of rural communities and informal borrower networks built on TRUST that many microlenders can’t or won’t serve.

Financial Literacy

We have embedded financial literacy as part of our process to encourage responsible borrowing. We educate the borrowers on how to improve their credit scores and improve their wealth status.

Credit Scoring

The underserved and informal borrowers are our primary target, and most of them have received credit scores for the first time ever through our Credit Scoring Platform: Patascore.
