Our approach to meaningful financial inclusion is different

and this is what makes us unique.

Hybrid Approach

We have agents all over who are embedded in our value chain to help us reach and understand existing rural communities built on TRUST that many microlenders can’t or won’t serve.

Financial Literacy

We have embedded financial literacy as part of our process to encourage responsible borrowing. We educate the borrowers to focus on wealth creation and not over indebtedness.

Credit Scoring

The underserved and informal borrowers are our primary target, and most of them have received credit scores for the first time ever through our Credit Scoring Platform, Patascore.

Our Technology

Our target audience are based in rural and low income communities, we have therefore built low tech solutions i.e. SMS, USSD that allows everyone and anywhere to access our services on the demand side
