Why We Exist

Pezesha comes from combining two Swahili words, Pesa and Wezesha to mean financial empowerment. To facilitate meaningful financial inclusion to the underserved communities through data and financial literacy while matching lenders with quality borrowers. Pezesha exists to give choice, inclusion and affordable digital financial services to low-income borrowers in Africa to bring freedom, equality and hope to them and their communities to be part of the formal financial system.

Our Mission

To facilitate meaningful financial inclusion to the underserved communities through data and financial literacy while matching lenders with quality borrowers.
