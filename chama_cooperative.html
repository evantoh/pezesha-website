Chama to Chama Lending Platform

Pezesha is transforming existing informal social networks across Africa to be more efficient in their already existing lending activities.

What we offer Chamas & Co-operatives

We are in the business of automating relationships not transactions. This way we create a strong network effect that is based on TRUST.

Microlending Marketplace

You can choose to lend to only your Chama members or lend to other Chamas in our network. By lending through our safe and trusted marketplace as a chama/ Co-operative you will generate more returns of at least 20% annually through our platform.
