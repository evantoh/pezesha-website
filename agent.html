Become a Pezesha Agent Today

Start making money by becoming a Pezesha agent in your community or location.

  • First get a credit score from Patascore
  • Send us your credit score e.g. 600
  • Have a smartphone
  • Proof of a good track record in your residential area/community
  • Must be part of a Chama or a trusted network in your community
  • Have or knows of an existing trusted networks of low income borrowers running micro businesses in your area
  • Must have experience working with low income groups/Chamas
  • Have a high level of integrity, professionalism and credibility as part of their core values
  • Fluent in Swahili
  • Willing to refer and grow other agents within their networks

Responsibilities

  • Identifying and vetting quality borrowers running micro businesses in low income communities, preferably where they reside
  • Administering financial education to Pezesha customers
  • Assist in on boarding and consolidating new quality borrowers through Pezesha application process
  • Loan collections and following up on borrowers
  • Assist in collection of additional data sets as required during and post loan application
  • Be the brand ambassador of Pezesha by creating and retaining trusted relationships within their areas of focus.

Compensation

  • Commissions based on performance on recruitment of quality borrowers and their timely repayments
  • Other benefits: Financial education training, more returns from being a lender on our marketplace, access to affordable loans.
