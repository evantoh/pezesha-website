What is a Credit Score?

A credit score is an expression based on an individual’s credit files that is used to determine their creditworthiness - if the individual qualifies for a loan, at what interest rate, and what credit limits - A credit score basically assesses a person’s ability to repay a loan.

What is Patascore?

Patascore is a financial literacy tool and a highly accurate, consistent and robust Credit Scoring platform.

✓ It educates borrowers on their financial standing
✓ It trains and informs borrowers on how to repay their loans, why they should repay their loans and in time.
✓ It shows borrowers the steps they should take in order to improve their credit scores.
