Why We Exist

Pezesha comes from combining two Swahili words, Pesa and Wezesha to mean financial empowerment. Pezesha exists to give choice, inclusion and affordable digital part of the formal financial system

Our Mission

To facilitate meaningful financial inclusion to the underserved communities through data and financial literacy while matching lenders with quality borrowers.
